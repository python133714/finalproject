Help & FAQ

Find quick answers and support resources for using Event Manager.

How do I create an event?

Click the "Create Event" button on the Events page (you must be logged in). Fill out the form and click "Save event" to add it to your list.

Where is my data stored?

This demo stores data locally in your browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data will remove stored events and accounts.
